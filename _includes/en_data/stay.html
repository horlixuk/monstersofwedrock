Where To Stay

Several tyeps of accomodation are available at Rosedew farm. The self contained stable blocks are reserved for the wedding party and their immediate family. Also available are Lodges, Yard (shepherd) huts, glamping and camping. Let Izzi and Mark know if you'd like to stay in a Lodge or Yard hut. To book camping or glamping ring 01446 794024 or email info@acorncamping.co.uk.
