Frequently Asked Questions

What will the weather be like?

November in Wales is likely to be wet and a little chilly. We suggest you bring some stout shoes and an umbrella.

What is the dress code?

Its festival clothing! Seriously though, wear whatever you want - we are!

Will transportation be arranged?

You'll need to make your own way to and from the venue. Let us know if this is a problem and we can help.

Where can I eat nearby?

Old White Hart, The Cwtch, Illtuds Restaurant

Where can I have breakfast on Sunday

Cwtch Cafe, Acorn Camping

Does my ticket include a plus one or children?

One ticket per person! Please exchange it for a lanyard when you arrive. Your lanyard will show which table you are on.

When and how should I RSVP?

Please email rsvp@monstersofwedrock.co.uk by the 7th July.

Do I need to bring a gift?

No! If you would like to contribute then please consider a donation to

English Springer Spaniel Welfare

What if I feel unwell?

To protect our VIP OAPs, please dont come if you feel poorly. You dont want to be patient zero do you? We'll make it up to you somehow!
